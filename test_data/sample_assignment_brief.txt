BUS3012 SERVICE MANAGEMENT
GROUP ASSIGNMENT BRIEF — SEMESTER 2, AY2025/2026

OVERVIEW
This group assignment requires you to conduct a service quality audit of a real-world service organisation using the SERVQUAL framework. Your team must apply at least THREE of the five SERVQUAL dimensions (Tangibility, Reliability, Responsiveness, Assurance, Empathy) in your analysis.

DELIVERABLES
1. Written Report (60%)
   - Minimum 4,000 words, maximum 5,500 words (excluding references and appendices)
   - Must follow APA 7th Edition citation format
   - Font: Times New Roman 12pt, 1.5 line spacing, 2.5cm margins
   - Must use the official report template (available on LumiNUS under "Assignment Resources")
   - Sections required:
     a. Introduction and Problem Statement
     b. Literature Review (minimum 10 peer-reviewed sources, published 2015–2025)
     c. Methodology (sampling method, survey instrument design, data collection procedure)
     d. Findings and Discussion (statistical analysis required — SPSS output must be included as appendix)
     e. Conclusion and Recommendations
     f. Peer Evaluation (each member rates all others on contribution, included as separate appendix)

2. Presentation (40%)
   - 12–15 minute group presentation during Week 13 tutorial slot
   - Slides must be submitted to LumiNUS 24 hours before presentation
   - All members must speak for a roughly equal amount of time

SUBMISSION DEADLINE
Written Report: Sunday 18 April 2026, 11:59 PM (Singapore Time) via LumiNUS
Presentation slides: Friday 16 April 2026, 11:59 PM
